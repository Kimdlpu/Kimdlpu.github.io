Xiang Chen (陈翔)

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. candidate in Intelligent Media Analysis Group (IMAG) at Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST) , supervised by Prof. Jinshan Pan. My research interest includes image dehazing, image deraining and related low-level computer vision problems.
